Free Puzzle iPhone & iPad 4+

Sharpen your observation skills in mysterious scenes packed with cleverly hidden objects. Unlock levels, earn stars, and prove how fast you can spot the unseen. Mystery Places Hidden Objects is a visually rich seek-and-find adventure—every scene challenges your focus, speed, and attention to detail.

How to play

  • Study each scene carefully.
  • Find every object on the list.
  • Finish before the timer runs out.
  • Earn stars based on speed and accuracy.

Game features

  • Beautiful, detailed mystery locations.
  • Multiple levels with rising difficulty.
  • Star ratings to track how you perform.
  • Locked levels that open as you progress.
  • Hints when you need a nudge.
  • Simple tap controls.
  • Built to train focus and observation.
  • An internet connection is required for gameplay.
