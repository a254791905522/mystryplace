Scope

This policy describes privacy practices for Mystery Places Hidden Objects, the mobile game distributed on Apple’s App Store. It is for general information only and should be read alongside Apple’s own privacy disclosures for the App Store, iCloud, and your device.

On the App Store product page, the developer has indicated that this app does not collect data in the categories covered by Apple’s App Privacy labels. Specific practices can still depend on app version, features, and how the operating system handles diagnostics or crash reporting.

No names, email addresses, or similar identifiers appear on this promotional site. This page does not use analytics or advertising trackers.
